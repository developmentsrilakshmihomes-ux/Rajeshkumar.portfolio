Our Story

Leadership Journey

Two decades of milestones that define who we are today.

2012
Company Foundation

Sri Lakshmi Homes Pvt. Ltd. was established in Chennai with a vision to provide legally compliant, value-driven residential plots. Built on trust, transparency, and customer satisfaction, the company began its journey in the real estate industry.

2012–2015
Early Growth Phase

The company successfully launched its initial residential plotting projects, earning customer confidence through clear documentation, quality development, and timely project execution. These early successes laid the foundation for future growth.

2016–2019
Regional Expansion

As Chennai's infrastructure expanded, Sri Lakshmi Homes strategically entered emerging growth corridors. The company increased its project portfolio and strengthened its presence across key residential destinations with high investment potential.

2020–2023
Landmark Projects

The launch of flagship developments including Lakshmi Lake View, Dhanalakshmi Nagar, Lakshmi Green City, and Lakshmi Modern City marked a significant milestone. These CMDA & RERA-approved projects reinforced the company's reputation for quality and reliability.

2023–2024
Awards & Recognition

With over 1,250 happy customers and CREDAI membership, Sri Lakshmi Homes earned recognition as a trusted real estate developer known for transparency, customer satisfaction, and legally secure investments.

2025 & Beyond
Current Success & Future Vision

Today, Sri Lakshmi Homes stands as a leading plotted development company with 12+ years of experience. Guided by a vision to serve 10,000+ customers by 2029, the company continues to create thriving communities and investment opportunities across Tamil Nadu.
